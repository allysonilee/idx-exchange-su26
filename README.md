# IDX Exchange - Data Analyst Internship (Summer 2026)
## About
Analyze real-world MLS transaction data through weekly deliverables over a 12 week program.
## Week 1
Retrieve and respectively concatenate CRMLS sold and listing data from January 2024 to the most recently complete calendar month. Save new CSVs consisting of exclusive residential property types. 
## Week 2
Inspect the dataset and filter to include only relevant residential property records by removing columns with high amounts of missing data/
## Week 3
Merge the concatenated sold and listing datasets with the FRED MORTGAGE30US series on a monthly key.
